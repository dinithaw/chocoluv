About Our Small Business...


ChocoLuv is a chocolate company that was founded in 2022 by a group of students from NSBM Green University. We are a team of 7 members who are passionate about chocolate and we are here to provide you with the best chocolate experience.

We are a hardworking, youthful, and dynamic crew dedicated to give our best to you. Our goal is to revolutionise the way we buy and select our favourite chocolates. This platform enables consumers not only in Sri Lanka, but all across the world, to order and enjoy hassle-free delivery to one's door step.
